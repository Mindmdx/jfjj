助力中国竞技滑雪接轨国际顶尖水平

目前，国内竞技滑雪的发展情况亦如热爱她的那群人一般，朝气蓬勃却经验不足，富有激情却难以释放，渴望成长却 时常迷茫，可说那雪板上少年的身影正是国内竞技滑雪发展的缩影————年轻，充满无限可能！

Currently, just as the zealous young racers shredding on their skis, Chinese Ski Racing Industry is potent yet unexperienced, vibrant yet constraint, growing yet easily diverted. Just as the speeding figures of youth, it is saturated with possibility.

Tripeak Ski Racing希望成为沟通国内外竞技滑雪的桥梁，并通过运用世界顶级竞技滑雪资源为国内的青少年们构建 起最专业的竞技培训体系，为中国竞技滑雪的未来贡献一份力量。

The core missions of Tripeak Ski Racing is to establish links between international ski racing and the domestic of China. We hope to introduce top notch race training techniques to the young Chinese Racers, hoping to advance the industry with professional skills. By doing so, Tripeak Ski Racing wishes to contribute all we have to the promoting future of Chinese Ski Racing.
